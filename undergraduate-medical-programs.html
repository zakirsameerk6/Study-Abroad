Undergraduate Medical Programs

Available Programs

MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery)

Duration: 5.5 years

Overview: Comprehensive program covering various aspects of medicine and surgery, preparing students for a career in healthcare.

BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery)

Duration: 5 years

Overview: Focuses on dental science and practices, providing the necessary skills and knowledge to become a professional dentist.

BPT (Bachelor of Physiotherapy)

Duration: 4.5 years

Overview: Specialized program in physiotherapy, equipping students with skills to help patients improve their physical movement.

B.Sc Nursing

Duration: 4 years

Overview: Program designed to train students in various aspects of nursing, patient care, and medical practices.
